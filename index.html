But beyond the work, I'm genuinely curious about how the world works: why some organizations thrive while others stagnate, what makes a good decision under uncertainty, and how AI is quietly reshaping industries most people haven't noticed yet.

This site is where I share what I find interesting — through writing, through the work I do, and through the perspectives I've built up over years of working in complex environments.
