Who I am

Son of refugees.
Strategy nerd.
Proud dog dad.

My parents came to Denmark as boat refugees from Vietnam. They arrived with almost nothing. That's probably why I work hard and never take opportunities for granted — because I've seen what it looks like when you start from zero and build something real anyway.

I finished my Masters in early 2024, spent time at Strategy& consulting, and ended up at Norlys — Denmark's largest integrated energy and telecom company — first as a Manager, now as Senior Manager working on AI transformation and product strategy. I'm 28. Things have moved fast.

Right now I'm also navigating a new apartment in Copenhagen with my girlfriend Cecilie and our dog Aika. Adult life is chaotic and I highly recommend it.
