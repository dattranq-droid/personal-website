Always Moving · Always Hungry · Always Late to the Airport

Born with
wanderlust.

Travel is the thing outside work I spend the most time thinking about. New cities, new food, new ways people organise their lives — I find all of it genuinely fascinating. Which might explain the number of trips to Switzerland. From Wroclaw in March 2023 to wherever I go next — the full story is below.

The first boys trip — the debut of what would become the core Aarhus group. Wroclaw, a vodka tour, and a man we only ever knew as Dr. Vodka. I didn't know that weekend would become a template for the next few years. The best kind of accident.

Italy
Jun 2023 · Trip #2
Milano
📷 Add photo
Our first trip together — technically before we were a couple, but only just. My first time in Milano, and I immediately fell for it. The aperitivo, the Duomo, the way everything looks effortlessly right. The city has a lot to answer for, in the best possible way.
Aug
Portugal
Aug 2023 · Trip #3
Lisboa, Porto & Lagos
📷 Add photo
Our second trip and first time in Portugal. Pastéis de nata every morning, pastéis de bacalhau at every opportunity, and driving in Lisbon — which is genuinely unhinged. Hills, trams, narrow one-way streets, and everyone somehow doing it perfectly. Portugal won immediately.
Aug
Switzerland
Aug 2023 · Trip #4
Zurich & Engelberg
📷 Add photo
First time in Zurich — the beginning of what would become a personality trait. Then up to Engelberg for my first real hike. You take the cable car to 3,000 metres and the world gets very quiet and very large. Switzerland did something to me on this trip.
Oct
Vietnam
Oct 2023 · Trip #5
Ho Chi Minh City
📷 Add photo
Taking Cecilie to visit our home country — my grandmother, my parents, the family that made me. She met all of them. And I watched her fall in love with Vietnam, which made me fall in love with it all over again too. The most meaningful trip on this whole list.
Nov
South Korea
Nov 2023 · Trip #6
Seoul
📷 Add photo
Visited my sister during her exchange semester and ended up staying for the food. So much Korean barbecue. So much kimchi. Grills going at every table. Also an important discovery: statistically, Koreans have very large heads. I don't know what to do with this information, but I think about it often.
Nov
Switzerland
Nov 2023 · Trip #7
Zurich
📷 Add photo
Back in Zurich for the winter market. Glühwein in hand, Christmas lights everywhere, the whole city in a very satisfied mood. Switzerland in December has decided that winter should feel like a reward, and they are completely right about that.

First trip with Cecilie's family. No pressure. Budapest delivered on every front — beautiful city, thermal baths, ruin bars, food that made everyone happy. Managed to impress across the board. I consider this an unqualified win.

France
Mar 2024 · Trip #9
Paris
📷 Add photo
Hadn't been since I was a baby, so this was really my first time. And it lived up to everything. The food is exactly as good as they say. The vibes are exactly as real. I stood in front of a painting and something clicked. Paris is the only city on this list where I can genuinely imagine living. That surprised me.

One of my favourite destinations. Full stop. The food, the culture, the craftsmanship — the quiet intentionality behind absolutely everything. Tokyo made me understand what a city looks like when it actually tries. I came back different. I'm going back.
