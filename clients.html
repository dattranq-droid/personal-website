Always Moving · Always Hungry · Always Late to the Airport

Born with
wanderlust.

Travel is the thing outside work I spend the most time thinking about. New cities, new food, new ways people organise their lives — I find all of it genuinely fascinating. Which might explain the number of trips to Switzerland. From Wroclaw in March 2023 to wherever I go next — the full story is below.

🇵🇱 Poland
Mar 2023 · Trip #1
Wroclaw
📷 Add photo
The one that started everything. Wroclaw in March — cold pavements, a colourful market square, and hundreds of hidden dwarf statues dotted around the city. I didn't know this weekend trip would turn into four years of barely sitting still. The best kind of accident.

📷 Add photo
Milano taught me that Italians take aesthetics seriously in a way that kind of changes how you see everything else. The aperitivo culture. The Inter shirt. The beginning of a very expensive habit.

Portugal in three cities — Lisbon's hills, Porto's wine cellars, Lagos's cliffs into the Atlantic. One of those trips you measure everything else against. Portugal just does it right.

Zurich & Engelberg

Engelberg sits at the foot of Mount Titlis. You take a cable car up and suddenly you're standing in a completely different world — 3,000 metres of perspective. Highly, highly recommend.
