Always Moving · Always Hungry · Always Late to the Airport

Born with
wanderlust.

Travel is the thing outside work I spend the most time thinking about. New cities, new food, new ways people organise their lives — I find all of it genuinely fascinating. Which might explain the number of trips to Switzerland. Below is the full list, from where it started (Milano, June 2023) to wherever I went most recently.

Rome
The eternal city. Pasta, ruins, and the kind of chaos that somehow works perfectly. Also the best carbonara I've ever had — and I've had a lot.
🇬🇧
Nov 2026
London
Grey skies, great food, and an inexplicable urge to apologise constantly. Also the Tate Modern at 9pm with no queue, which should be illegal.
🇨🇭🇮🇹
Aug 2026
Road trip — Zurich → Dolomites → Garda → Bologna → Torino → Milano
One of those trips where you plan loosely and it somehow ends up being exactly right. Mountains, lakes, and more pasta than is medically advisable. Ten out of ten, would carb-load again.

🇨🇭
Jul 2025
Zurich & Klosters
Switzerland again, because apparently it's just going to be a theme in my life. Klosters is the kind of village that makes you question your Copenhagen apartment decisions.
🇪🇸
Mar 2025
Málaga & Marbella
Sun in March. That's really all you need to say. The Costa del Sol does what Danish winters simply cannot. I came back five shades less pale and significantly happier.
🇵🇹
Mar 2025
Lisboa
Back again. Lisbon has a way of pulling you back — the light, the pastéis de nata, the trams that are definitely not designed for modern body proportions.
🇨🇭
Mar 2025
Zurich
Third Zurich of the year. At this point I'm basically an honorary citizen. The Swiss have figured something out — I'm still not entirely sure what it is, but I respect it.
🇨🇭
Feb 2025
Zurich
Cold outside, warm inside. Switzerland in winter has its own kind of charm — the kind that involves a lot of cheese, mulled wine, and morally questionable amounts of raclette.
🇩🇪
Jan 2025
Berlin
Berlin in January — brutally cold, utterly cool. The city where no one cares what you do, in the best possible way. Also the best döner I've had outside of Turkey. Possibly in the world.
2024
🇵🇹
Oct 2024
Lisboa
Autumn in Lisbon is genuinely perfect. The crowds thin out, the light gets golden, and the wine stays cheap. The hills stay steep, unfortunately. My calves have opinions about Lisboa.
🇨🇭
Aug 2024
Zurich & Lauterbrunnen
Lauterbrunnen is what screensavers are based on. Waterfalls everywhere, mountains blocking the horizon, air that smells like nothing has ever been wrong in the world. Surreal.
🇯🇵
Jul 2024
Tokyo
Nothing prepares you for Tokyo. The scale, the organisation, the food, the vending machines, the food again. I came back with a deep respect for Japanese culture and a mild existential crisis about Danish infrastructure.
🇻🇳
Jun 2024
Ho Chi Minh City, Hội An & Huế
Back to Vietnam — this time longer, slower. Hội An is everything they say it is. Huế surprised me completely. This trip felt personal in ways I'm still processing. Recommended to literally everyone.
🇮🇹
May 2024
Milano
Second time in Milano — the city grows on you. Fashion week stress very much not included. Just good coffee, better aperitivo, and architecture that makes Copenhagen feel like it's still figuring things out.
🇵🇱
Apr 2024
Warszawa
Warsaw is more beautiful and more complex than I expected. A city that's rebuilt itself — literally and figuratively — and carries its history without being crushed by it. Criminally underrated.
🇫🇷
Mar 2024
Paris
Yes, Paris. Always Paris. I know it's a cliché, but standing in front of the right painting at the right time does something to you. Also: the bread. The bread is not a cliché. The bread is simply correct.
🇭🇺
Feb 2024
Budapest
Budapest in winter is moody and magnificent. Thermal baths, ruin bars, and the kind of coffee culture that makes you want to sit and think for three hours. I understand why people move here.
2023
🇨🇭
Nov 2023
Zurich
First of many Zurich trips. The Swiss have figured something out and I'm not entirely sure what it is — but I keep going back to try to understand. Clean streets. Excellent public transport. Exceptional cheese.
🇰🇷
Nov 2023
Seoul
Seoul moved faster than any city I've ever been to — in every sense. The food, the energy, the nightlife, the skincare stores every five meters. A city that genuinely doesn't waste a single moment.
🇻🇳
Oct 2023
Ho Chi Minh City
First time back since I was a kid. Walking through the streets knowing my parents once walked these same streets — with very different plans for their future — hit differently. Still thinking about it.
🇨🇭
Aug 2023
Zurich & Engelberg
Engelberg sits at the foot of Mount Titlis. You take a cable car up and suddenly you're standing in a completely different world — 3,000 metres of perspective. Highly, highly recommend.
🇵🇹
Aug 2023
Lisboa, Porto & Lagos
Portugal in three cities — Lisbon's hills, Porto's wine cellars, and Lagos's cliffs straight into the Atlantic. One of those trips you measure everything else against. Portugal just does it right.
🇮🇹
Jun 2023 · Where it started
Milano
Where the journey starts. Milano taught me that Italians take aesthetics seriously in a way that kind of changes how you see everything else. The beginning of a very expensive habit.
