The career story

Where I've been & what it meant.

A timeline with actual notes — not just titles and dates.

Work Experience
March 2026 — Present
Senior Manager, Product Management & AI Business Transformation
N Norlys · Copenhagen

Norlys is Denmark's largest integrated energy and telecom group — 1.7M customers, 4,500+ employees. I lead product management practices and work on how AI is reshaping the way we operate and build things.

I got this role because I'd spent the previous year watching our product practices up close, getting quietly frustrated, and apparently talking about it too much — so they gave me a job to fix it. I got my PSPO I Scrum certification before starting because I had imposter syndrome and needed to feel ready. Turns out you still feel like an imposter after. But at least I know what a sprint is.

May 2025 — March 2026 · 11 mos
Manager, Strategy & Transformation (Channels)
N Norlys · Copenhagen

Led a strategically prioritised program with 10+ cross-functional projects and 75+ stakeholders, central to Norlys's company-wide transformation. Drove operational alignment across customer service, digital channels, and back-office processes — translating long-term ambitions into measurable outcomes. Executive-level reporting to senior leadership and close collaboration with business, IT, operations, and commercial teams.

Going in-house after consulting is a culture shock nobody really prepares you for. As a consultant, you leave at the end of the engagement. In-house, you live with your decisions — and with the people you made them with. It's harder in a better way. I genuinely loved this job.

February 2024 — May 2025 · 1 yr 4 mos
Associate
S& Strategy& (PwC) · Copenhagen

Consistently rated Impact Tier 1 — the firm's highest performance rating, awarded to a very limited number of consultants each year. Strategy consulting on corporate strategy, transformation, and operating model work across healthcare, public administration, energy & utilities, automotives, and retail — both domestic and international engagements.

Specialised in integrating data-driven analysis with strategic consultancy, with extensive experience transforming service operations: significant cost reductions while simultaneously enhancing customer experience and increasing sales.

Strategy& is where I learned how to structure a problem, present to people who don't have time for your full analysis, and work fast without cutting corners. Also confirmed that I preferred building things over advising on them — which eventually pulled me toward Norlys.

August 2022 — February 2024 · 1 yr 7 mos
Junior Associate
S& Strategy& (PwC) · Aarhus

Conducted research, data analysis, and built recommendations for clients across industries. Worked closely with senior consultants to deliver strategy projects — first real exposure to how rigorous thinking actually gets applied inside real organisations.

The role that changed everything. Joining Strategy& as a student was the most formative period of my career. Watching how world-class consultants structure problems and present to C-suites — this is where I became who I am professionally. Transitioning from Junior Associate to Associate was the promotion I'm most proud of.

June 2020 — August 2022 · 2 yrs 3 mos
CEO & Founder
H Hyree (Self-employed) · Copenhagen

Built and launched Hyree — a peer-to-peer marketplace app connecting people with local helpers for small practical household tasks: furniture assembly, mounting, moving, and more.

  • Top 10 on the Danish App Store
  • 3,000+ registered users
  • Enrolled in Beyond Beta startup accelerator

Two and a half years of learning what it actually means to build something from scratch. Product development, user research, sales, fundraising, failure, and iteration — all of it. I learned more in those years than in any classroom. I still miss the chaos a little bit.

March 2019 — June 2020 · 1 yr 4 mos
Junior Business Analyst
NU Nuuday · Denmark

Managed B2B customers in cases of advance contract resignation. Developed new processes for monitoring, calculation, and charging of claims. Built benchmark reports and trained new employees on optimised processes.

Tools: SQL, VBA Excel, Power BI.

My first job. Nuuday had just been spun out of TDC Group and was figuring things out — I was figuring things out too. Where I first learned that the gap between how organisations say they work and how they actually work is enormous. Also where I learned SQL. Both equally formative.
