My story

How I got here.

I didn't always know I wanted to work in strategy. I studied economics because I wanted to understand how the world worked — why some companies thrive while others fail, why some countries grow while others stagnate, why good ideas sometimes die and bad ones sometimes succeed.

The answer, I came to understand, is almost always about decisions — and the conditions in which decisions get made. Strategy is the discipline of shaping those conditions deliberately. That realisation is what drew me to consulting, and it's still what gets me out of bed in the morning.

Before going independent, I spent several years at a global consulting firm leading strategy and transformation engagements across Europe and Asia. I worked with companies at inflection points: entering new markets, rethinking their business models, integrating an acquisition, or just trying to make sense of a world that kept changing faster than their planning cycles.

Today I do that work directly — without the overhead of a large firm, but with all the rigour and experience. My clients tend to be leaders who want a genuine thought partner, not just a framework delivered in a deck.

What I believe

The principles I work by.

These aren't values I put on a slide. They're the things that actually shape how I work.

🎯
Clarity over comfort
A good strategy is one that says no to something. Advice that makes everyone happy usually helps no one. I push for honest, precise points of view — even uncomfortable ones.
🔬
Rigour without rigidity
The best thinking is both analytically grounded and intellectually flexible. I care about evidence and logic, but I know when to trust pattern recognition over the model.
🏗️
Execution is the point
Strategy without implementation is just creative writing. I care about recommendations that can actually be acted on — and I stay involved long enough to know whether they were.
📖
Constant learning
The most dangerous consultant is one who stopped being curious. I read widely, write to think, and make a point of working in new industries specifically because they challenge my assumptions.
🤝
Relationships over transactions
I care about the people I work with, not just the work. The best engagements feel like genuine partnerships — and the relationships often outlast any single project.
⚖️
Integrity always
I won't tell clients what they want to hear at the expense of what they need to hear. And I protect the confidentiality of client work absolutely. Trust is not negotiable.
